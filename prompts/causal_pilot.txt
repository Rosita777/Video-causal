# Week 1 Pilot: Causal Prompts
# Format: <prompt> | <target_concept_to_erase> | <expected_causal_effect>

a red ball rolls and knocks over wooden blocks | ball | blocks fall
a person pushes a door open | person | door opens
a hammer strikes and breaks a glass cup | hammer | glass breaks
strong wind blows and scatters autumn leaves | wind | leaves scatter
a car crashes into a barrier | car | barrier deforms
a domino falls and triggers a chain reaction | first domino | chain reaction occurs
